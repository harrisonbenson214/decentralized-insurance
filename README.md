# Decentralized Insurance Platform

## Overview

A peer-to-peer insurance platform built on the Stacks blockchain that enables automated claim processing and policy management without traditional insurance intermediaries. This platform provides transparent, community-driven insurance coverage with smart contract automation.

## Smart Contracts

### 1. Policy Management Contract (`policy-management.clar`)
- **Purpose**: Create and manage insurance policies and premiums
- **Features**: Policy creation, premium calculations, coverage management, policy lifecycle tracking

### 2. Claims Processor Contract (`claims-processor.clar`) 
- **Purpose**: Automated claim verification and payout system
- **Features**: Claim submission, automated verification, payout processing, fraud detection

## Key Features

- **Peer-to-Peer Insurance**: Direct coverage between community members
- **Automated Claims Processing**: Smart contract-driven claim verification
- **Transparent Pricing**: Algorithm-based premium calculations
- **Community Governance**: Decentralized decision-making for policy terms
- **Fraud Prevention**: Advanced verification and reputation systems
- **Instant Payouts**: Automated claim settlements upon verification
